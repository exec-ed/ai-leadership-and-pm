How to Get the Most Out of This Day

  1. Come ready to think hard. These exercises aren't lectures‚Äîyou'll be making real decisions with limited information.
  2. Engage with your group. The learning happens in the debate, not just the conclusions.
  3. Use this site as a reference. If you need to review a case brief, role card, or handout during the day, pull it up here.
  4. Ask questions. If something isn't clear, the facilitator is there to help you think through it, not give you the answer.
  5. Take notes on the handouts. They're templates for thinking, not finished products.
